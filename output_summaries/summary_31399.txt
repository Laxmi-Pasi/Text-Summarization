Energean Leading E&P player in the   Eastern Mediterranean Energean’s operations began when the Group acquired the Prinos  licences, offshore North East Greece, in 2007. At the time,  these licences contained 2 mmboe of audited 2P reserves. Ten years later, the Group has built up a balanced portfolio  of producing and development assets, containing a total of  51 MMbbls of oil 2P reserves in Greece; 1.2 Tcf of natural gas  2C resources in Israel; and 39.4 MMbbls of oil 2C resources in  both countries. Energean is committed to realising the significant  growth potential of its development and exploration projects,  such as the Karish and Tanin fields, offshore Israel, and other  licence blocks in Greece and Montenegro.
