The power of N Brown Group plc  Annual Report & Accounts 2017 An expert in fashion that fits and  flatters, N Brown is one of the UK’s  leading online retailers. Our key retail  brands are JD Williams, Simply Be  and Jacamo. We are all about democratising fashion and are size inclusive,  focusing on the needs of underserved customer groups – size  20+ and age 50+. We offer an extensive range of products,  predominantly clothing, footwear and homewares, and our  Financial Services proposition allows customers to spread the  cost of shopping with us. We are headquartered in Manchester where we design,  source and create our product offer and we employ over  2,700 people across the UK.
