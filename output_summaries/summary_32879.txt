STMGROUPPLC.COM ANNUAL  REPORT &  ACCOUNTS  2017 ANNUAL REPORT & ACCOUNTS 2017 02 STM Group Plc is a multi-jurisdictional financial services group listed on the  Alternative Investment Market of the London Stock Exchange. The Group specialises  in the delivery of a wide range of financial service products to professional  intermediaries and in the administration of assets for international clients in  relation to retirement, estate and succession planning and wealth structuring. Today STM has trading operations in the United Kingdom, Gibraltar, Jersey, Malta  and Spain.
