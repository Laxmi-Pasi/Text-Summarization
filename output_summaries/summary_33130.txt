EDC solutions are used to enable off-line data and documents gain an online status, to automatically extract mission critical information and link the content to a broad range of IT applications. Through authorised business partners DICOM Group has helped more than 10,000 blue chip enterprises in over 60 countries to accomplish superior office efficiency. Its customers are supported by more than some 700 EDC specialists working out of one of its 23 subsidiaries in Europe, the US, South East Asia and Australia.
