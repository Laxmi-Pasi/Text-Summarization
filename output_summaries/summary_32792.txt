Wizz Air Holdings Plc Annual report and accounts 2017 6  STRATEGIC REPORT  COMPANY OVERVIEW CONTINUED  History of the Group  Wizz Air was founded in 2003 by its current Chief Executive Officer (CEO) József Váradi and five other  individuals who recognised a demand for low-cost carriers in CEE driven in particular by the accession of ten  new EU member states on 1 May 2004, eight of which are in CEE (the Czech Republic, Estonia, Hungary, Latvia,  Lithuania, Poland, Slovakia and Slovenia), and the anticipated accession of Bulgaria and Romania to the EU in  January 2007..
