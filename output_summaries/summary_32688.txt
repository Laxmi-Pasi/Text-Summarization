Redefine International is a diversified   FTSE 250 UK-REIT committed to becoming  the UK’s leading income focused REIT. Our income-led business model and strategic   priorities are designed to offer shareholders   superior , sustainable and growing income returns. Redefine International holds a primary listing on the  London Stock Exchange and a secondary listing on  the Johannesburg Stock Exchange and is included  within the FTSE 250, EPRA and GPR indices.
