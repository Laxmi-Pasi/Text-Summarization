Annual Report and Accounts 2017 Every day, people around the  world live, work and play on  ground prepared by Keller. Used alone or in combination,  our techniques solve a wide  range of geotechnical  challenges across the entire  construction spectrum. We are the world’s largest  geotechnical solutions  specialist. Our projects are  typically for a single, local  site, perhaps for a building,  a basement or a bridge. We have the financial strength,  know-how, capacity and the  global reach to tackle some  of the largest and most  demanding projects around  the world. Whatever the size of the  project, we have the people,  expertise, experience and  financial stability to respond  quickly with the optimum  solution, execute safely  and see it through to  a successful conclusion.
