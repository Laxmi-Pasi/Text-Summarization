We own 826 funeral locations  and operate 45 crematoria in the United Kingdom and have a strong market presence in pre-arranged funeral plans, where people plan and pay for their funeral in advance. We are here to help people at one of the most diﬃcult times in their lives and we are honoured to serve the local communities we are part of with expertise, compassion and total commitment. We strive to set the highest possible standards in our facilities and in the funeral service and care we provide. Delivering excellent client service in an evolving digital age and in an increasingly competitive funeral sector is core to our strategy. In this way we are well positioned to provide the best quality service at each price point and market segment we choose to compete in.
